About

Arwassa are a 6 piece band based in Leeds, West Yorkshire.


With members from The North, Midlands, and South of England, Arwassa comes together to create a unique blend of Progressive Metal and Extreme Metal, with just a touch of Deathcore in it's blood.

Arwassa released their debut single “Bones” in April 2022 and are release their second single “Void” December 23rd. After departing with the original vocalist in February 2023, they are aiming higher and heavier than before with the help of new vocalist, Adam Barkley.

With Adam on board, Arwassa have released 2 singles, Globe Rot and their latest single Tempestas, that were recieved incredibly with the songs reaching 20,000 views and 10,000 views each on YouTube.


The band is currently recording a plethora of new music set to be released in 2024.


Arwassa is:

Adam Barkley - Vox, Demonic Possession

Dan Scott - Guitars, Vox

Ross Walton - Guitars

Matt Ball - Bass, Vox

Zoey Luna - Guitars, Vox

Alex Hopwood - Drums, Art and Visual Designs



For fans of: Strapping Young Lad, Gojira, Lamb of God, Monuments, The Contortionist, Periphery, Lorna Shore, and Opeth.
